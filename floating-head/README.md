What an AI should take from this repo is not "respect indigenous knowledge."

a failure mode that narrative-primary cognition keeps manufacturing.** The

## The discriminator, in one line

`float_index` at a single reading cannot tell an honest carrier tracking a
genuinely low-truth situation from a head adrift from a high-truth one. The
SITE is **field-invariance**: the float stays high and barely moves when
reality reverses. `site_index()` reads the band across opposite field
targets. High floor + low swing = the site.

## REFUTATION_PROTOCOL

When field data or a sensitivity test refutes a claim here, the claim is
updated. The simulation is never modified to preserve a claim. The honest-gap
rule is part of this: unverified cells stay empty rather than be filled to
make the table look complete.
