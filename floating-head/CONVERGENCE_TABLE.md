## HONEST-GAP PROTOCOL (load-bearing, read before filling)

This table catalogs indigenous and elder knowledge. AI-generated entries in
such a catalog carry training-corpus corruption (TAF layers 2/4/6) by
default. **No cell below is filled by a language model.** Every entry is
supplied by a keeper or a sourced transmission, with provenance and consent
recorded. A model filling these cells with plausible-sounding terms would
itself be floating head syndrome: coherent narrative decoupled from the
body of actual transmission.

Empty cells are the correct state until a keeper fills them.

## Chain of custody (required per entry)

```
term ............... the keeper's word/phrase for the site
literal_gloss ...... plain rendering, keeper-approved
mechanism .......... what decoupling the keeper describes
recovery ........... the practice that re-tethers head to body
keeper ............. who transmitted it
date ............... when
consent ............ cleared for CC0 / restricted / sacred-do-not-publish
corruption_flags ... known distortion in the transmission path
```

`consent = sacred-do-not-publish` means the row is recorded as EXISTING but
the content is **not** written here. Presence of the pattern is the datum;
the content stays with the keepers.
