## Troubleshooting Guide

### Low Phosphorus Extraction

- Check pH levels (target 6.5)
- Verify electrode connections
- Clean electrode surfaces
- Increase residence time

### Insufficient Energy Generation

- Check diffraction grating condition
- Verify electrical connections
- Adjust electrode angle
- Clean photoelectrode surface

### Temperature Too High

- Reduce power density
- Increase hotspot spacing
- Improve water flow
- Add cooling mechanisms

### Wildlife Concerns

- Verify temperature at 1m distance
- Install warning signs if needed
- Adjust hotspot locations
- Increase monitoring frequency
