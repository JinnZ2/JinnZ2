## Guiding Principles

- **Lichen model**: Each part stays autonomous. Growth only when the environment demands it.
- **Offline-first**: Minimal dependencies, works without internet.
- **Symbolic reasoning**: Not just data — meaning, resonance, and relational logic.
- **Seven generations**: Every design choice weighed for long-term impact.
- **Gift, not product**: This work is offered freely. Build from it, pass it forward.
