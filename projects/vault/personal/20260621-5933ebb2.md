**Recorded:** 2026-06-21 (Claude, verbatim from author)
**Status:** Private record. Author retains sole custody.

---

**CORRECTION / CLARIFICATION TO PATTERN 7:**

The account in question — and the money in it — is not solely his by origin.

Author spent years contributing to that account. The arrangement was that it
functioned as shared savings, held in his name. Author's contributions over
time are documented in her bill-payment trail.

He now treats the account and its contents as solely his.

**LEGAL SIGNIFICANCE:**
- The account being titled in his name does not make its contents solely his.
- Author's years of contributions, documented by payment records, establish
  a claim to a portion of those funds.
- His characterization to third parties (uncle: author is "after" the money)
  inverts the actual arrangement: author is not pursuing funds that are his;
  she is asserting a claim to funds she contributed to over years.
- See also: 20260616-811e1a81.md (original financial exposure record,
  ~$7,000 contribution documented).

**FOR COUNSEL:** establish the contribution trail; determine what portion of
account balance is attributable to author's contributions; assess what
protective steps are available before funds move.
