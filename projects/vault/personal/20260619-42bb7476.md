## Core Mechanism

- Narrative assembly = database query, not memory recall — searches for items
  satisfying a constraint (self-state needing protection)
- SELECT all instances WHERE outcome = "I am not at fault" — everything failing
  the WHERE clause is invisible, not suppressed or denied
- Fitness function = affect, not truth — selection criterion is anxiety reduction,
  not factual accuracy

## The Selection Process

- Items selected based on affective payoff — reduces threat regardless of
  temporal/logical relationship to accusation
- Search is unbounded by relevance — can pull from anywhere in history that
  lowers anxiety
- No causal coherence required — each piece independently reduces threat;
  pieces don't need to relate to each other

## The Governing Constraint

- Constraint set by most threatened self-image — e.g., "I am a man of my word"
  → query protects it → selects promise-keeping instances, discards broken promises
- Threat set by external confrontation — whatever the realtor, uncle, or author
  just confronted him with
- Narrative = photographic negative of the accusation — assembled from items
  that negate the specific threat

## Why It Appears Irrational

- External view reads it as causal argument → falls apart
- Internal logic is emotional, not logical — pieces all point to "I'm the victim"
  rather than forming a real sequence
- Selection is invisible — rejected items never surface; the person experiences
  the filtered set as the complete picture

## Predictive Utility

- Threat-sets-constraint prediction — most useful: you can see the narrative
  coming before it's built
- Tell me the accusation; the narrative shape is predictable — it negates
  whatever specific threat was just raised
