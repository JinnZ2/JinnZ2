This is a self-sealing defensive loop with three structural components:

**1. The reflexive inversion**
The initial observation (pattern X) is not met with engagement but with a
meta-claim about the observer's motive (hostility through obscurantism). This
shifts the topic from the content of X to the character of the one naming X.

**2. The double-bind creation**
The responder frames the act of naming as inherently coercive (i.e., "you are
making me irrational"). This imposes a forced choice: withdraw the observation
(validating the deflection) or persist (validating the accusation of coercion).
Both outcomes serve the defender's preferred reality.

**3. The retroactive proof-text**
Any subsequent reaction from the observer — whether escalation, clarification,
or silence — is retrofitted into the defender's original framing as evidence of
aggression. The loop closes because the defender's premise becomes unfalsifiable;
all counter-moves are pre-interpreted as symptoms of the very offense they claim
to be resisting.

---

**Net effect:** A conversational trap where the original pattern remains
unexamined, and the burden of proof is permanently shifted onto the observer
to disprove a motive they never held.
